Socks are worn on feet. In Korean, it is written as 양말. They work in many ways, absorbing sweat, stopping shoes from rubbing feet and keeping warm. Socks in sandals have usually been regarded as bull-shxt styling. However, recently, some people wear socks in that way for dressing up. They are categorised according to the length, long socks, middle socks, short socks, trainers socks and invisible socks. Like red, green, blue, purple, black and white, socks have a variety of colours. They are not woven but knitted. They are manufactured in a column-shaped machine with cotton, spandex, rubber and etc. They have many patterns like flowers, plants, animals, geometric lines or dots, text, unknown abstract shapes or no pattern. People purchase socks offline or online by themselves and give them as gifts to friends and family, especially on Chirstmas.
